HAE:ON — Account & Data Deletion

Last updated: July 5, 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your HAE:ON account and associated data. HAE:ON is operated by Entanglecare.

How to request account deletion

  1. Send an email to entanglecare@gmail.com with the subject line "HAE:ON Account Deletion Request".
  2. Include the email address linked to your account (if you signed up with email), or describe your account (device model and approximate signup date) if you used anonymous sign-in.
  3. We will verify the request and complete the deletion within 30 days, and confirm by reply email.

What is deleted

  • Your account record and login credentials (email address, if provided)
  • All chat messages and story progress with the App's characters
  • Gameplay state: bond levels, items, collections, endings, and CG gallery unlocks
  • Wallet balance and gem transaction history
  • Push notification identifiers

What is kept, and for how long

  • Purchase records required for tax, accounting, and fraud-prevention obligations may be retained for up to 5 years as required by applicable law, then deleted.
  • Aggregated analytics that do not identify you are retained (they cannot be linked back to your account).

Partial data deletion

If you want some of your data deleted without deleting your whole account (for example, clearing chat history), email us at the address above describing what you'd like removed. We will process the request within 30 days.
